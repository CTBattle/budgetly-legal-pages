# Budgetly Privacy Policy

_Last updated: January 11, 2026_

Budgetly ("we", "us") helps users track spending and manage budgets. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect
- Account and transaction data when you connect a financial account using Plaid
- Account metadata such as balances, institution name, and transaction history
- Basic identifiers used to provide the service
- App diagnostics to improve stability

## Plaid
Budgetly uses Plaid to connect financial accounts. We do not receive or store your bank login credentials.

## How We Use Information
- Provide budgeting tools and financial insights
- Display accounts, balances, and transactions
- Improve reliability and prevent abuse
- Provide customer support

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with required service providers such as Plaid.
