# Budgetly Privacy Policy

_Last updated: January 11, 2026_

Budgetly ("we", "us", or "our") helps users track spending and manage budgets. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

## Information We Collect
- Account and transaction data when you connect a financial account using Plaid
- Account metadata such as balances, institution name, and transaction history
- Basic identifiers used to provide the service
- App diagnostics and usage data to improve stability and performance

## Plaid
Budgetly uses Plaid to connect financial accounts. We do **not** receive or store your bank login credentials. Plaid handles authentication directly with your financial institution.

## How We Use Information
- Provide budgeting tools and financial insights
- Display accounts, balances, and transactions
- Improve reliability, security, and prevent abuse
- Provide customer support

## Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. Data is shared only with required service providers such as Plaid and infrastructure providers necessary to operate the App.
